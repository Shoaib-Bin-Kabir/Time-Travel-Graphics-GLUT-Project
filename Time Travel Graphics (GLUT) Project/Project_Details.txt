A graphical representations of three era's of time including a time portal : 
1. Egyptian Era
2. Medieval Era
3. Apocalyptic Era

Start with clicking 'Portal.exe' from the Time_Portal Folder 


==== Changing Scenarios ===
Press '1' Apocalyptic Era
Press '2' Egyptian Era
Press '3' Medieval Era
Press '4' Time Travel
===================================
=== Egyptian Era Controls ===
Boat 1 Movement: 'a' = Left, 'd' = Right
Boat 2 Movement: 'j' = Left, 'l' = Right
Toggle Day/Night: 'n'
====================================

=== Medieval Era Controls ===
Press W to move the cart
Press Q to stop the cart
====================================

=== Apocalyptic Era Controls ===
Press W to start car
Press Q to stop car
Press E to move the tank to left
Press R to move the tank to right
Press F to shoot from tank
Press S to move the tank barrel to left
Press D to move the tank barrel to right
====================================

=== Time Portal Scene Control ===
Press Q to stop human
Press W to move Again
Press E to move Human faster
Press R to move Human slower
Press T to turn on/off UFO light
Press Y to Abduct Human
Press O to turn on UFO drive mode
Press I to go up
Press N to go down
Press J to go left
Press K to go right
Press P to turn off drive mode
====================================
